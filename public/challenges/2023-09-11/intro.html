Given an array of integers, sort them into two separate sorted arrays of even and odd numbers. If you see a zero, skip it.

Example:

> separateAndSort([4,3,2,1,5,7,8,9])
> [[2,4,6], [1,3,5,7,9]]

> separateAndSort([1,1,1,1])
> [[], [1,1,1,1]]
