Given a positive integer n, return all of its anti-divisors. Anti-divisors are numbers that do not divide a number by the largest possible margin (1 is not an anti-divisor of any number). More information here!

Example:

> antidivisor(1)
> []

> antidivisor(3)
> [2]

> antidivisor(5)
> [2,3]

> antidivisor(10)
> [3,4,7]

> antidivisor(234)
> [4,7,12,36,52,67,156]
