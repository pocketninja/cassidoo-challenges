(thanks to Will for sharing it): Given a list of strings arr, and a max size n, return a new list where the strings (from left to right) are joined together with a space, so that each new string is less than or equal to the max size.

Examples:

> combineStrings(["a", "b", "c", "d", "e", "f", "g"], 5)
> ["a b c", "d e f", "g"]

> combineStrings(["a", "b", "c", "d", "e", "f", "g"], 12)
> ["a b c d e f", "g"]

> combineStrings(["alpha", "beta", "gamma", "delta", "epsilon"], 20)
> ["alpha beta gamma", "delta epsilon"]
